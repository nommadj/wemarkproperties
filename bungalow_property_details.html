Description

Welcome to Paazuri Residence, an exclusive beachfront housing project meticulously developed by Wemark Properties. Situated along the enchanting Malindi-Watamu Beach road in the tranquil setting of Casuarina, this distinguished community offers a total of 60 units comprising three-bedroom bungalows and maisonettes. Each residence occupies 700 square meters of prime coastal land, providing ample space for luxurious living.

Proudly boasting a strategic location, Paazuri Residence is a mere 6-minute drive from the bustling hub of Malindi CBD and less than 10 minutes from the convenience of Malindi International Airport. Maisonettes within the development feature three-bedroom suites with DSQ for added convenience, while bungalows offer master bedrooms with DSQ, all designed to cater to the discerning needs of modern coastal living. With maisonettes offering approximately 210 square meters of living space and bungalows approximately 150 square meters with flat roofs, Paazuri Residence promises a lifestyle of unparalleled elegance and comfort by the sea.
