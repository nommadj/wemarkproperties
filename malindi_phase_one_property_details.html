Description

Azizi Gardens in Baolala, Malindi, offers 50 x 100 prime plots for sale at 99,000 KES per plot. These plots are located along the Salagate tarmac road, approximately 20-25 minutes drive from Malindi Town.

The neighborhood is serene, secure, and well-developed, making it ideal for residential or commercial development. The land features a flat terrain and fertile soil, with ready title deeds provided. The plots are part of a well-planned community with flexible payment options available.
