Who We Are

Wemark Properties is a Real Estate company offering Prime, Affordable & Legit commercial, residential, agricultural and beach properties ideal for every investor.

We are currently selling prime properties in Malindi and Nairobi Metropolitan.

We also have our flagship Beachfront housing project in Malindi town dubbed Paazuri Residence


Founded in 2020 by Brian Onyango Otieno

We cater to diverse needs by offering commercial, residential, agricultural, and beach properties, ensuring every investor finds the ideal choice at affordable rates.

Why Choose Wemark?

Empowering Smart Property Decisions

At Wemark, our mission is to empower consumers with the information they need to make smart decisions regarding property purchase and ownership. We provide comprehensive insights and guidance to ensure your investment is sound and beneficial.

Extensive Experience

With over 5 years of experience, we have been dedicated to selling genuine properties, helping Kenyans settle in the coastal regions of the country. Our deep knowledge and expertise in the local real estate market ensure you receive top-notch service.

Value Addition

We go beyond just selling properties. All our offerings come with essential value additions such as water, electricity, murram roads, and fencing. These enhancements make our properties not only desirable but also ready for immediate use.

Genuine Title Deeds

Your peace of mind is our priority. All our properties undergo thorough due diligence, guaranteeing clean and genuine title deeds. When you choose Wemark, you choose security and transparency.
